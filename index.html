Student experience of Mauritius

Life at the tropical island is an exhilarating experience that marinates the learning journey.
Discover the life that stimulates positive mental health state to achieve excellence.
← →
Port Louis
Port Louis

The busiest city in the Indian Ocean is a perfect place for student life. The big secret little known to many is the amazing graffiti in Port Louis; an inspiration to build creativity for students. The booming market is a perfect place to go shopping at affordable prices and unwind from the normal school environment.

Grand Baie
Grand Baie

Do you want to party? Do you want to have fun? Do you want to enjoy the tropical sandy beaches? Grand Baie is the perfect place. The place is filled with restaurants and lively bars. Incredible beach activities in Pereybere public beach are the go-to destination for most students since it is near the campus with lots of fun activities to have a social life away from school.

Le Morne
Le Morne

Where are the hikers? Le Morne is a fantastic hiking destination adjacent to the Ocean in the southern part of Mauritius. Taking a road trip to the South and hiking the Le Morne mountains is a perfect activity after a busy week riddled with submissions. The viewpoint is a wonderful place for photoshoots while enjoying the beautiful ocean waves and the sunset.

Flic-en-Flac
Flic-en-Flac

Do you want to relax, play with sand and swim? Or do you want to party and mingle with diverse ethnic groups? Flic en Flac is a cross breed of both. It is one of the beautiful sandy beaches on the island booming with tourists. Welcome to Flic en Flac the flat land!

Pamplemousses
Pamplemousses

The botanical garden is the jewel of Pamplemousses. The perfect place to go for walks with your girlfriend. Unfortunately for the singles, you’ll have to walk with your mosquito repellents coz they’ll definitely feast on your warm blood. Fun activities like treasure hunts are conducted at the botanical garden. A mahogany mall is also a perfect place for lovers to have a date night viewing the beautiful lights at the manmade lake.

Caudan Waterfront
Caudan Waterfront

The perfect place for date nights with friends and lovers. The beautiful lights and reflection from the adjacent ocean attract ALU students to spend their leisure time in this exhilarating place. The Cinema theatre spices more on the leisure and fun one can get at Caudan Waterfront. The beautiful Odysseo Oceanarium is both a fun place and a harbour of knowledge to widen one's imagination.
