Advice to prospective students

Focus on yourself. Look up. Watch out! You might find yourself graduating earlier than your body and mind thought you would.

—Pacifique Rubasha

Have clarity of what you want to achieve and how to achieve that. Know the single step that you need to take in that a thousand mile journey. Work from known to unknown. In case of uncertainty, consult widely from your peers, mentors and coaches.

—Emmanuel Okorwoit

show your leadership initiatives and desire to do hard things.

—Noel Oduory

Expect the unexpected and learn how to move about.Life on the island is fun depending on your preferences,places like Casela,chamarel,black river gorges national park will give you a thrill of the island.

—Harry Goliyo

Don't let the language barrier put you off going to ALU if you're a French speaker. I was once there as well. Don't be concerned if you have doubts about your English writing abilities. Simply search for free English online courses and take a few courses while you wait for your first term to begin or whenever you have time.

—Adja Gueye

Enjoy, be yourself and have fun; you only live once at the end of the day and continue to do hard things.

—Mussie Teferi
